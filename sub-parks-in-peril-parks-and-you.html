Parks And You

What parks do you have in your local neighbourhood? How big are they? What ecosystem services do they provide? Do they face any threats? And what biodiversity do they support?

Many parks are supported by volunteers or 'friends' groups. You can join one or start your own! If you dont have a park close by, lobby your local council to create one, or transform your back yard into a biodiversity mini-park!

Perhaps the best thing you can do about your local park is to be proud of it tell your friends and family about the fascinating creatures that live there and what a good place it is to visit. Make it the topic of your next school project, or even make it a career by becoming a conservation biologist.

Ecosystem Services

These are the services that parks and other green areas provide, like helping to give us pure water, fertile soil, food and medicines.
