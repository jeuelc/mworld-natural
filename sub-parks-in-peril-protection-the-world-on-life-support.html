The World On Life Support

Did you know that the world has a life support system? This system doesn't need lots of wires and machines to work though, its in the trees, oceans, animals and soil!

The varied plant life on Earth helps to keep the air and water healthy and pure, provides pollinators to help crops grow and keeps the soil fertile. Having diverse ecosystems can even help to reduce infectious diseases and control pests.

Ecosystems in and outside of our parks keep our climate nice and steady by absorbing and storing carbon from the atmosphere. They can also affect local rainfall and play a big part in cycling nutrients through the soil. Have you ever noticed that it gets cooler the further away from a city you travel?

Plants and animals outside protected areas do these jobs too, but by having lots of healthy parks we can make sure that many species will be around in future to sustain us as the world's population grows.
