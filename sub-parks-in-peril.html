Parks In Peril

When we think of protected areas, many people think of beautiful mountains, pristine jungles and rare animals. But protected areas can be almost anything, from a stunning coral reef to your backyard!

Protected areas or parks might be completely isolated from humans, or people might live in them and depend on their resources to survive.

Come with us to see how we are working together to protect the untouched parts of our environment and learn about some incredible protected areas around the world. You might even learn that there is a fascinating park near you!
