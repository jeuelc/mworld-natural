Geology: It's a Piece of Cake!

All rocks are the same...right? Well, no... and we know this by the work of geologists (scientists who study how the earth works), who over the last few hundred years have been studying rocks and fossils to piece together the history of the Earth.

Most rocks that form on the Earth's surface are made up of layers, just like a cake... but not quite as tasty! Within these layers are written the secret history of our remarkable planet.

Tap the buttons to discover how you can read Earth's fascinating story from its rocks.
