Reef, Rhinos and Re-Introductions

Some parks are created to protect spectacular or scientifically important landforms, like the Grand Canyon in America or Uluru in Australia. Some protect culturally important or sacred sites.

Other parks protect biodiversity and people come from all over the world to see the amazing animals and plants that thrive there.

Come and explore some of the most diverse parks around the world and learn about their challenges and triumphs.
